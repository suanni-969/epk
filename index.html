Artist Bio

Suanni is a techno DJ and producer blending industrial pressure, dark groove, and bounce. Inspired by Berlin's underground and ancient myth, her sound is raw, driving, and ritualistic built for late night warehouses and high energy dance floors.

Having studied music in Berlin and lived in New York City, Suanni brings a global perspective to her sets, moving between fast, punishing rhythms and sensual tension to create an immersive experience that feels both modern and timeless.

Suanni is a San Francisco based DJ and producer delivering high energy hard bounce and groove driven techno built for movement, release, and collective intensity. Shaped by time studying music production in Berlin, her sound draws from underground warehouse culture, late night dance floors, and fast, pressure driven club energy.

Her sets blend hard bounce, hard groove, industrial textures, and hard techno, driven by punchy low end, rolling percussion, and hypnotic, bouncy rhythms. With a strong focus on flow and momentum, Suanni designs her sets around cycles of tension and release locking bodies in and keeping the floor moving without distraction.

Suanni has performed across clubs, warehouses, open air renegades, and underground events, supporting artists such as Deborah De Luca, ØTTA, Funk Tribu, Southstar, Somewhen, Franck, Dani Thorne & MCYL.

Expect fast tempos, physical grooves, and an atmosphere built for sweat, movement, and release.

🐉 The Mythology: Suanni's name is inspired by the mythical 狻猊 (Suanni), a dragon lion associated with smoke, fire, patience, and power. That mythology is reflected in her sound: controlled intensity, cinematic build ups, and relentless momentum.
